In the spirit of Valentine's Day, I thought it would be rather funny and neat if I could find a way to make an installation that poked fun at love in one way or another. Coincidentally, I had seen a clip online of two circles arguing with each other about why they couldn't be together because they were unable to love each other. It was kind of weird but also funny, so I decided to do something in this sort of spirit for my project. Thus, A Love Story About Two Circles was born and I set off on making my vision come to life!
I began by bringing one of my circle friends to life. Using some functions from the TFT Graphics Library, I was able to draw a circle who, sitting in the corner of the screen, would look up and ask their fellow shape: "Do you love me?"

From here I was in a little bit of a problem. I knew my own jokes to this probably wouldn't be that funny, so with the help of some friends, I made a list of potential responses that the other circle could give. The plan was to let the ESP randomly choose which one it would say at random. To me this supplied part of the generative element to the piece, as the jokes/answers varied in topic. Some classmates reacted with comments like "It's like you're watching a tiktok with a different punchline every time," which I thought fit the spirit of the piece as a lighthearted fun way of using technology to tell a story about two shapes who may or may not be in love. This was a fun way of getting familiar with the ESP32 microcontroller and the TFT Graphics that came with the screen. While I ultimately settled on simple shapes, the opportunity to explore what the library had to offer was a great way of preparing myself for the rest of my adventures with the ESP32, particularly because installing the library to begin with was a challenge in itself! As my classmates had also experienced, it was difficult to get the dependencies to communicate with the ESP32 was a little bit of a challenge, especially on older hardware. In all, it was a great time. And I enjoyed making jokes about two circles in love--that part was fun too! Unfortunately I didn't get a video :^'( Sorry!
